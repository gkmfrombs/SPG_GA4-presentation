Potential Solutions: What Should Stefan Do?

Observer take on experts view and pros and cons explanation- By Rohit Kumar

1. Direct Confrontation

  • Honest & Clear
  • Addresses the root problem
  • High risk Vijay quits
  • Goes against leadership's past advice

2. Strategic Appeal

  • Appeals to Vijay's ego
  • Frames change as a challenge
  • Lower risk of immediate conflict
  • May be too indirect to work

3. Escalate & Wait

  • Gets leadership buy-in
  • Creates a unified strategy
  • Lets the problem fester
  • Shows weakness to Vijay
